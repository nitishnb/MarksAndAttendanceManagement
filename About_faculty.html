Overview:


Attendance Management System is a web-based application developed for daily student attendance in schools, collages and institutes. It facilitates to access the attendance information of a particular student in a particular class and also the marks of students on basis of each subject. The information of student, faculty , courses are stored and sorted by the admin. This system will also help in evaluating attendance eligibility criteria of a student. This system basically has two main modules: 1)First module is admin which has right for creating space for new batch of students. Any entry of new faculty and entry of courses info are done by admin. 2)Second module is handled by the user which can be a faculty. Faculty has a right of making daily attendance report and also can enter marks of the students in particular subject, handled by user(or faculty) The attendance status and marks updation can be viewed by students by logging into their respective account.



Motivation:


The importance of school education lies in the fact that the children of today will become adult citizens of tomorrow. The growth and future of our country highly depends upon the quality of the present school/college education system. And in a good school/college the chief objective is to stimulate interest and curiosity in the students and provide all possible facilities for headmasters, teachers and parents to achieve the desired goal which is a better and easier educational ways for students. The main motivation of developing attendance management system is to computerized the tradition way of taking attendance. Taking attendance record for each student is to be calculated. And gives report for each student. In this way, this system reduce paperwork and save time and money with mobile attendance management system. It also eliminate duplicate data entry and errors in time and attendance entries. Improve visibility to track and manage student attendance. Real-time status tracking of attendance. Track the information of teachers and staff, manage allocation. Auto-generate various types of reports of class or student attendance. Increased security and confidentiality with role-based permissions to users.
